About Us
Vision
In alignment with Saudi Vision 2030, specifically the Green Riyadh project, we envision a flourishing Riyadh, where communities actively collaborate for a greener, sustainable future, enhancing urban spaces and fostering well-being for all.

Mission
Empowering Riyadh's communities to champion environmental sustainability through tree-planting, volunteerism, and education, united by our platform towards a greener tomorrow.
